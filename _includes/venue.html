RSE'26 will be hosted at the Institut de Robòtica i Informàtica Industrial (IRI), a joint research institute of the Spanish National Research Council (CSIC) and the Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya (UPC), located on the UPC campus in Barcelona, Spain.

Sessions will take place in the Labora lab at IRI, which comfortably accommodates 30–50 participants. If additional space is required, a function room at the nearby Escola Tècnica Superior d'Enginyeria Industrial de Barcelona (ETSEIB) will be arranged.
