# Travel Information

RSE'26 will be held at the **Institut de Robòtica i Informàtica Industrial (IRI), CSIC-UPC**,
C/ Llorens i Artigas, 4-6, 08028 Barcelona, Spain.

Information on how to reach Barcelona and the venue will be added once dates are confirmed.
In the meantime, the accommodation options below are already available.

For any urgent queries contact the organizers at [rsemeeting@gmail.com](mailto:rsemeeting@gmail.com).

---

## Accommodation

The options below are within convenient reach of IRI. Prices and availability should be checked directly with each property.

### University Residences & Research Accommodation

These options are particularly suited for researchers and are located close to the UPC campus:

- **[Torre Girona University Hall of Residence – RESA](https://www.resa.es/en/halls-of-residence/barcelona/torre-girona/)** — On-campus university residence, walking distance from IRI.
- **[Resa Barcelona Diagonal](https://www.resa.es/en/halls-of-residence/barcelona/barcelona-diagonal/)** — Modern student/researcher residence on Avinguda Diagonal.
- **[Col·legi Major Penyafort – Montserrat](https://www.penyafort-montserrat.com/)** — University hall of residence near the UPC campus.
- **[Residència Universitària Aleu](https://www.fundacioaleu.org/)** — University residence close to the Diagonal area.
- **[Residència d'Investigadors (CSIC)](https://www.residenciainvestigadors.com/)** — CSIC's own researcher residence in central Barcelona; particularly recommended for attendees.

### Hotels

A selection of hotels in the area, ranging from mid-range to higher-end:

- **[ApartHotel Atenea Barcelona](https://www.hcchotels.com/en/hotel-atenea-barcelona/)** — Serviced apartments near the venue.
- **[Hotel Hesperia Barcelona Presidente](https://www.melia.com/en/hotels/spain/barcelona/hesperia-barcelona-presidente)** — Well-located hotel on Avinguda Diagonal.
- **[HCC Saint Moritz](https://www.hcchotels.com/en/hotel-saint-moritz/)** — Boutique hotel in the Eixample district.
- **[HCC Regente](https://www.hcchotels.com/en/hotel-regente/)** — Centrally located on La Rambla.
- **[Hotel Àmbit](https://hotelambit.com/)** — Design hotel in the Sant Antoni area.
- **[Hotel Silken Ramblas](https://www.hoteles-silken.com/en/hotels/silken-ramblas-barcelona/)** — On La Rambla, well connected to the rest of the city.
