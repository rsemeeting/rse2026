# Travel Information

RSE'26 will be held at the **Institut de Robòtica i Informàtica Industrial (IRI), CSIC-UPC**,
C/ Llorens i Artigas, 4-6, 08028 Barcelona, Spain.

Information on how to reach Barcelona and the venue will be added once dates are confirmed.
In the meantime, the accommodation options below are already available.

For any urgent queries contact the organizers at [sfoix@iri.upc.edu](mailto:sfoix@iri.upc.edu).

---

## Accommodation

The options below are within convenient reach of IRI by walking or public transport. Prices, availability, and eligibility should be checked directly with each property.

### University Residences & Research Accommodation

These options are particularly suited for researchers and are located close to the UPC and Diagonal / University Zone area:

- **[Torre Girona University Hall of Residence – RESA](https://resa.es/en/residences/barcelona/torre-girona/)** — Located on UPC Campus Nord and one of the most convenient options for walking to IRI.
- **[Resa Barcelona Diagonal](https://resa.es/es/residencias/barcelona/barcelona-diagonal/)** — University residence near Avinguda Diagonal with good public transport connections.
- **[Col·legi Major Penyafort – Montserrat](https://www.ub.edu/allotjament/)** — University hall of residence in the Zona Universitària area, convenient for the venue.
- **[Residència Universitària Aleu](https://yugo.com/es-es/global/spain/barcelona/aleu?utm_source=GMB&utm_medium=map)** — Modern university residence in the University Zone, close to UB and UPC faculties.
- **[Residència d'Investigadors (CSIC)](https://www.residencia-investigadors.es/en/accommodation/)** — Research-focused accommodation in central Barcelona; suitable for academics but not adjacent to the venue.
